Our story

Dallas's Premier Electrical & Solar Company

To provide safe, reliable, and energy-efficient electrical solutions that exceed expectations while treating every customer like family.

Supreme Electric and Solar was founded on the belief that quality electrical work shouldn't come with a premium attitude. Our owner, a licensed master electrician with over 15 years in the trade, started the company to offer Dallas homeowners and businesses a better choice: transparent pricing, punctual service, and workmanship that stands the test of time. What began as a one-truck operation has grown into a trusted team serving thousands of satisfied customers across the metroplex.

Today, we're proud to be a leading provider of electrical repairs, panel upgrades, EV charger installations, and solar energy systems. Our commitment to continuing education and code compliance means every job is done right the first time. We give back to the community through free safety inspections for seniors and sponsorships of local youth sports teams. At Supreme Electric, we're not just electricians—we're your neighbors.
