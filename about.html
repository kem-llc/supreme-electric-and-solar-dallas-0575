Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electrical & Solar Partner

To provide safe, reliable, and innovative electrical and solar solutions that power the lives of our neighbors in Dallas.

Supreme Electric and Solar was founded with a simple mission: deliver top-quality electrical work with honesty and transparency. Starting as a small two-person operation, we quickly earned a reputation for reliability and craftsmanship. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service electrical contractor serving hundreds of homes and businesses across Dallas.

Our team of licensed electricians and solar specialists undergoes continuous training to stay ahead of industry advancements. We believe in building lasting relationships through exceptional service, upfront communication, and a commitment to safety. Whether it's a minor repair or a major solar installation, we treat every project with the same dedication.
