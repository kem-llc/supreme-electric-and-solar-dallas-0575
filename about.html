Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electrician Since 2008

To deliver safe, reliable, and innovative electrical solutions that power homes and businesses while exceeding customer expectations.

Supreme Electric and Solar was founded in 2008 by a veteran electrician with a vision to provide Dallas with honest, high-quality electrical services. Starting from a small truck, we built a reputation for reliability, transparency, and craftsmanship. Over the years, we expanded into solar energy, helping homeowners and businesses harness Texas' abundant sunshine.

Today, we are a family-owned team of licensed electricians and solar specialists serving the entire Dallas metroplex. We pride ourselves on same-day service, upfront pricing, and a lifetime workmanship warranty. Whether it's a simple outlet repair or a full solar installation, we treat every home like our own.
