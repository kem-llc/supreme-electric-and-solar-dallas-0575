EV Charger Installation

Professional installation of Level 2 EV chargers for your home or business.

Schedule this service

Overview

Charge your electric vehicle faster and conveniently at home or work. We install Level 2 charging stations from leading brands like Tesla, ChargePoint, and JuiceBox. Our electricians ensure proper wiring, breaker sizing, and code compliance. We also offer commercial charging solutions for businesses and multi-unit dwellings.

What's included

  • Faster charging – up to 25 miles per hour
  • Convenient overnight charging at home
  • Increase property appeal for tenants
  • Eligible for utility rebates

How it works

1

Site Assessment

We evaluate your electrical panel, parking location, and recommend the best charger.

2

Installation

We run conduit, install the charger, and connect it to a dedicated breaker.

3

Testing & Setup

We test the charger, show you how to use it, and register any warranties.
